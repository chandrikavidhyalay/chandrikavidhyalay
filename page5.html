About Us 

Chandrika Vidhyalay is a leader in education, where both learners and educators thrive. Our Students are unafraid to be awesome. We empower our students to serve and inspire the community and become strong global citizens and courageous, socially responsible leaders. At Chandrika Vidhyalay, we are inspired by hope and we respect, explore and experience the beauty and wonder of the God faith. The greatest professionalism exists throughout the school, always aimed at building a better future.

The School's purpose

  • What we do
    We empower amazing students to do amazing things. 
  • How we do things
    Our teachers are role models who inspire each students to reach her personal best. Their lessons last a lifetime. 
  • Why we exist
    Our holistic education underpinned by the God faith, helps our students thrive today and tomorrow.
